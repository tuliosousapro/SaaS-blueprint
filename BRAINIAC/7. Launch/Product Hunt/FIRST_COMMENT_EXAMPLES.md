## Pattern Breakdown

A strong first comment follows this structure:

1. **Personal greeting** — Address the community directly. Use first name and role.
2. **The "why"** — What problem drove you to build this? Make it relatable.
3. **Differentiator** — What makes your product different from existing solutions?
4. **How it works** — Brief, clear explanation. No jargon.
5. **Use cases** — Show real-world applications. Help readers see themselves using it.
6. **CTA** — One clear call to action (try it, visit, sign up).
7. **Engagement hook** — Invite questions and feedback. Stay available all day.
