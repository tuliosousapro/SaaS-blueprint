## Product Hunt Launch Checklist

**500 engaged leads = Top 5 of the day**

Warm accounts (active on PH) are worth more than cold accounts in the algorithm.

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks before)

### Ground Reconnaissance

- [ ] Create a personal Product Hunt account (not a brand account)
- [ ] Participate actively: vote, comment, discuss for 2+ weeks
- [ ] Study 5 successful launches in your niche
- [ ] Decide: self-hunt or external hunter (79% of featured are self-hunt)

### Objective Definition

- [ ] Define upvotes goal
- [ ] Define leads/signups goal
- [ ] Define traffic goal

### Launch Date

- [ ] Choose day: **Tuesday or Thursday** = more traffic (+15% clicks)
- [ ] Set launch to **00:01 PST** (05:01 Brasilia time)
- [ ] Verify there are no big competing launches on the same day

### Best Practices

- [ ] Complete all Product Hunt fields
- [ ] Clear, lean, clean visuals
- [ ] A short personal video
- [ ] Launch discount coupon
- [ ] Reply to all comments immediately
- [ ] Post in Build in Public on X:
    1. Day countdown
    2. Screenshots
    3. Small features
- [ ] Create SEO pages
    1. Comparison with competitors
    2. Alternative pages
    3. Content focused on frustrated users
- [ ] Ask for feedback from users requesting a refund and always do it immediately and fast.
- [ ] Set up retention, failed payment, and re-engagement email flows.
- [ ] Share on Reddit, on pages that make sense
- [ ] Show the Founder's face

---

## Phase 2: Arsenal Building (3-4 weeks before)

### Mandatory Assets

- [ ] **Title:** Product name only (no emojis, no descriptions)
- [ ] **Tagline:** Short, direct, clear value proposition (e.g.: "One workspace. Every team.")
- [ ] **Description:** Brief, USPs + main features
- [ ] **Thumbnail:** 240x240px — logo or animated GIF (not too busy)
- [ ] **Images/GIFs:** 1270x760px — benefits, not features
- [ ] **Video:** Product demo OR founder walkthrough (balance to not eliminate site visit)
- [ ] **First comment:** Story + feedback request (70% of #1s did this) — See [examples](FIRST_COMMENT_EXAMPLES.md)

### Pre-Written Messages

- [ ] DM template for warm leads
- [ ] DM template for cold leads
- [ ] Posts for Twitter/X (10 posts for the day)
- [ ] Posts for LinkedIn
- [ ] Email to user base
- [ ] Message for groups (Slack, WhatsApp, Discord)

**NEVER ask for upvotes directly.** Ask for support and feedback. If they like it, they vote.

---

## Phase 3: Lead Capture (2-4 weeks before)

### Leads Spreadsheet (Minimum 500)

- [ ] Friends and family (warm up their accounts on PH beforehand)
- [ ] Coworkers
- [ ] Followers on social networks
- [ ] **Upvoters of similar products** → use [upvote-bell.com](http://upvote-bell.com)
- [ ] Influencers you know
- [ ] Email/newsletter list
- [ ] Community members (Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, Facebook)
- [ ] Followers of the "Coming Soon" page

### Account Warm-up

- [ ] Instruct leads to use Product Hunt before launch
- [ ] Engage with potential supporters on Twitter
- [ ] Join relevant communities: Indie Hackers, Product Folks, etc.

### "Coming Soon" Page

- [ ] Create teaser on Product Hunt (up to 1 month before)
- [ ] Collect emails of interested people
- [ ] Generate early buzz

---

## Phase 4: Launch Day (D-Day)

### Execution Sequence

**00:01 PST (05:01 BRT)**

- [ ] Publish product
- [ ] Post first comment immediately

**First 2 hours (critical)**

- [ ] DMs to WARM leads first (higher conversion)
- [ ] Posts on all social networks
- [ ] Activate Product Hunt widget on website

**Hours 2-6**

- [ ] DMs to cold leads
- [ ] Messages in groups (Slack, WhatsApp, Discord)
- [ ] Emails to user base
- [ ] Contact with influencers

**Throughout the day**

- [ ] Reply to ALL comments on Product Hunt
- [ ] Post 10x on networks about progress
- [ ] Monitor competitors via [pw2.akkio.com](http://pw2.akkio.com)
- [ ] Engage with anyone who shares/comments

**Golden rule:** The first 4 hours are decisive. PH hides votes during this period — a good product gains organic traction.

---

## Phase 5: Post-Launch (D+1 onwards)

### Capitalization

- [ ] Publicly thank all supporters
- [ ] Reply to pending feedbacks
- [ ] Add Product Hunt badge to website
- [ ] Convert contacts into users/followers
- [ ] Implement feedback received in the product
- [ ] Document learnings for next launch

### Expected Results (if Top 5)

- ~10,000 unique visits
- 1,000-3,000 genuine leads
- Feature in Product Hunt digest
- SEO boost via backlinks
- Investor attention

---

## What NOT To Do

- [ ] Explicitly ask for upvotes
- [ ] Spam DMs all at once
- [ ] Use bots or manipulate votes
- [ ] Ignore negative comments
- [ ] Overthink — if you mess up, you can launch again
