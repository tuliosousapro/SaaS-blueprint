# Automations, Skills, and Tools: Distribution Phase

Put your product where your users already live.

## 1. SaaS Directory Submission Automations
- **Tool**: Robotic Process Automation (RPA) / Browser auto-fill
- **Use Case**: Get your SaaS listed securely on 100+ high DR directories (e.g., G2, Capterra, BetaList) without manually typing out forms.
- **Skill Implementation**: A centralized `project_meta.json` that populates a browser tool to rapidly submit profiles across targeted directories for SEO backlinks.

## 2. Community & Marketplace Tracker
- **Tool**: Slack / Discord API hooks
- **Use Case**: Monitor when your niche keywords are mentioned across relevant communities to naturally insert your product.
- **Skill Implementation**: An automated scraper or webhook alert that pings your internal channel whenever someone asks for a solution to the problem your SaaS solves.
