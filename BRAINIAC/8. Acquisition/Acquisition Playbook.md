# PLAYBOOK: First Customers (Acquisition)

> **Phase:** 8. Acquisition | **Priority:** P0 | **Est. Time:** 30 Days (Initial Sprint)

## Objective

Acquire your first 10-100 customers by executing a high-volume mix of reach-outs, community engagement, and organic content seeding.

## Deliverables

- [ ] Validated top 1-2 acquisition channels
- [ ] Published short-form content library (TikTok/Reels)
- [ ] Active cold outreach sequence (Email/DMs)
- [ ] Profiles on top industry directories

## Action Steps

## 🆓 The FREE Methods

### 1. Reach Out to Friends, Family, and Past Colleagues

Start with your warm network if your product is relevant to them. Even 1–2 warm users gives you a **testimonial**, a **case study**, and the **confidence** to go bigger.

> Warm relationships = leeway to figure things out without pressure.

### 2. Send DMs on Instagram, Substack, Twitter, and LinkedIn

**The formula:**

1. Find someone relevant
2. Do the work upfront
3. Show them a sample **BEFORE** asking for anything
4. Make it dumb simple for them to say yes

The OTHER way: have people DM **YOU**. Post content with a CTA like "comment X to get the free template" → automate with ManyChat.

### 3. Post on Facebook, Skool, and WhatsApp Groups

Every person in a niche group is **HIGHLY qualified**. They're already interested in the exact thing you do.

**The mistake:** Spamming and selling without ever contributing. Show up as a real member first. Answer questions. Share value. Then when you DO post about your product, people support it.

### 4. Post Short Content and Videos on Social Media ⭐

**The #1 mistake:** People try to be too clever with hooks.

- Scroll Instagram. See what's already going viral. **Copy the hook.** The title. The positioning. The colors. The font size.
- Pick **1 primary platform**. After you post, engage for the first hour.
- If a video gets more views than usual in hour 1, **double down on the winner**.

> This is the **#1 recommended method** for someone starting from zero.

### 5. Post Long-Form YouTube Videos

- Make the video about the **problem**, not your product. Your product shows up as the **solution** while you're teaching.
- Slower burn than short-form, but viewers are **MUCH more qualified** — they spent 20 minutes with you.

### 6. Send Cold Email

Use AI and automation to scrape emails (Google Business listings are a goldmine for local businesses). Write the value prop, AI personalizes at scale, and you send.

### 7. Submit to Directories

- [Product Hunt](/BRAINIAC/7.%20Launch/Product%20Hunt/Product%20Hunt Playbook.md) (hard to rank top 3 without existing community)
- [SubmitJuice](/BRAINIAC/7.%20Launch/SubmitJuice/SubmitJuice Playbook.md) (automates submissions to tons of directories)

More backlinks = better SEO = more organic traffic over time. **Set it and forget it.**

### 8. Revenue Share with Affiliate Partners

Find people who already have your audience. Offer them a cut of every sale.

**Key:** You want affiliates who **USE** your product and understand the space. Who feel the pain it solves. Who talk about it like it's their own.

### 9. Post on Relevant Product Forums

If your product integrates with another product, go hang out where **THEIR** users are. Post templates, answer questions, build credibility. Content stays up **forever** — people find it months later.

### 10. Rank in AI SEO for a Specific Keyword

Target **"Competitor + Alternatives"** keywords (e.g. "Buffer alternatives").

> People searching those keywords are **READY to switch**. They're already warmed up, educated, bottom-of-funnel.

### 11. Build Free Public Lead Magnets

Build a small, free resource (directory, curated list, spreadsheet). Your product appears **naturally** where it fits. No hard sell.

**Other ideas:**

- Curate 500 viral hooks for your niche
→ give the first 500 free
→ gate the next 500 behind email signup
- Does double duty: drives traffic **AND** collects emails

### 12. Offer Influencers a Webinar or Masterclass

Flip the script — instead of paying an influencer to promote you, offer to **HOST** a free high-value webinar for their audience. You bring the expertise, they bring the audience. **Win-win.**

### 13. Reverse Job Board with Upwork

Search for people looking for help with the problem your product solves. Pitch your tool or service as the solution. These people are **actively looking** and **willing to pay**.

### 14. Create a Free Custom GPT

If you have any kind of valuable dataset or framework, turn it into a free GPT. Takes an hour. Traffic compounds with zero ongoing effort.

### 15. Build Local Relationships

Chamber of Commerce. Local Facebook groups. Meetups. Become the "go-to person" in your local network. Some of the best first customers come from your neighborhood.

## 💰 The PAID Methods

### 16. Pay for Leads with Apollo

Search for specific types of people and companies, get their emails, reach out.

**Caution:** Start small. Test your messaging. Don't dump your budget into cold outreach before you've validated your value prop with free methods first.

### 17. Facebook Ads Funnel to Landing Page

- Start with a tiny budget: **$10–20/day**
- Test different hooks and landing pages
- **Don't scale until you see positive ROI**
- Fix the messaging with free methods first, THEN amplify with ads

### 18. Vibe Code Micro Apps to Drive Traffic

Build a small, free tool related to your product that people search for. It's like SEO but you're ranking a **TOOL**, not an article. ROI is wild once you rank for the right keyword.

### 19. Micro Press Coverage with HARO and ABNewswire

- **HARO** (Help a Reporter Out) — answer journalist calls for quotes → free backlinks and credibility
- **ABNewswire** — $80 press release targeting a specific keyword → start showing up in ChatGPT AI answers

### 20. Listen to Social Conversations with OGTool

AI monitors social media conversations (Reddit, Twitter, forums) and surfaces the relevant ones.

Use AI to **find** the conversations. Be a **human** when you engage. Share something personal. Be a little vulnerable. It stands out like crazy compared to bot-written responses.

### 21. Sponsor Niche Newsletters or Micro Influencers

- Go after **micro influencers** who understand your product and audience
- Someone with 1,000 TikTok followers has the same likelihood of going viral as someone with 1 million
- Start micro. Save your budget. **Get 5 customers, not 5 million impressions.**
