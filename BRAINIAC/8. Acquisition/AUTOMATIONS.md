# Automations, Skills, and Tools: Acquisition Phase

Drive scalable, non-linear traffic efficiently.

## 1. SEO Keyword Analysis & Content Clustering
- **Tool**: Ahrefs/Semrush APIs or custom scrapers
- **Use Case**: Automatically generate high-intent topical content maps instead of guessing keywords.
- **Skill Implementation**: Execute an AI pipeline that identifies long-tail competitor gaps and outputs a formatted 30-day content calendar for programmatic SEO.

## 2. Cold Email Sequencing Automations
- **Tool**: Instantly.ai / Lemlist Integrations
- **Use Case**: Scale personalized outreach directly to decision-makers without manual tracking.
- **Skill Implementation**: AI-generated cold email templates mapped to dynamic variables (e.g., scraping their LinkedIn for a personalized icebreaker), hooked into a lead delivery webhook.
