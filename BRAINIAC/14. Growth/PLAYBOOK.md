## Growth Idea Bank (37 High-Leverage Tactics)

Finding customers is one of the hardest parts about building a company. Here are 37 ideas that actually work:

1. **Expired domain goldmine.** Find high DA dead sites, rebuild with AI content. Rank for killer terms in weeks.
2. **Viral review bounty pages.** Add a page on your site “we pay for viral reviews.” $100 for 50k views, $200 for 100k. Cheaper than ads, 10x more authentic.
3. **Launch 1 micro-app per month.** Free. Cross-promote like crazy. Layer on paid ones after traction.
4. **AI search.** Run your entire category through ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity and list every question the models surface. Then create landing pages that answer those questions clearly.
5. **Make your product embedded in a framework.** Create a 5-step tactical playbook that solves a common pain in your market, and place your SaaS as one step inside the system.
6. **Craft killer weekly data reports.** The press eats it up. Turn these into slideshows on LinkedIn, Reels or TikTok.
7. **Build a “coaching layer” on top of your product.** Overlay AI guidance that teaches users how leaders in their role operate.
8. **Design a tiny path inside your product** that produces a visible outcome in under ten minutes. Highlight that win everywhere.
9. **Dead YouTube channel arbitrage.** Buy, update links, reupload. Huge lead gen on steroids.
10. **Free template downloads.** Notion, Shopify, Webflow templates for your use-case.
11. **Use AI to reshuffle your pricing into “jobs to be done” tiers.** Group pricing around outcomes instead of feature bundles.
12. **Build in public, but make it a game.** Feature voting board + leaderboard.
13. **Comparison site empire.** Launch niche “[Product] Alternatives” sites. Rank for competitor terms.
14. **“Best Of” industry directories.** Build top 100 lists for every niche in your industry. SEO goldmine.
15. **FOMO-driven invites.** “Get 1 month free, invite 5 friends, 24-hour expiry.”
16. **Chrome extension trojan horse.** Fix a popular app’s UI and funnel users to your product.
17. **Gamify everything.** Onboarding, referrals, feature unlocks.
18. **Turn customer calls into training data.** Use AI to transcribe calls and extract precise user language. Adjust copy to mirror their vocabulary.
19. **Ultra-premium upsell.** Launch a 10x price tier with concierge onboarding.
20. **Geo-arbitrage pricing.** Charge by local cost of living. Explode in emerging markets.
21. **Feature gate invites.** “Invite 3 friends to unlock this feature.”
22. **Cross app bundles.** Partner with non-competitors that share a customer base.
23. **Resource directory arbitrage.** Create resource libraries for adjacent industries.
24. **Study the best TikTok ads.** Recreate the structure for your niche.
25. **Interactive annual report.** Spotify Wrapped for B2B.
26. **Workflow teardown series.** Rebuild a broken company process using your product.
27. **Job change targeting.** Target people who just changed roles; fresh hires influence tool selections.
28. **Category glossary empire.** Publish the clearest glossary for your niche.
29. **Intent-mining through AI search.** Build landing pages that answer questions surfaced by LLMs.
30. **Niche leaderboard challenges.** Build a simple leaderboard tied to your product’s outcome.
31. **Local SEO/AI search arbitrage.** Create “[City] + [Pain Point]” landing pages.
32. **Affinity group takeovers.** Build micro landing pages for identity clusters (photographers, homeschool parents).
33. **Creator workflow packs.** Give rising creators three ready-to-use workflows.
34. **Hiring signal targeting.** Message teams hiring with a workflow that supports the exact role they’re adding.
35. **New LLC onboarding kits.** Scrape newly registered businesses and send founding startup toolkits.
36. **Negative keyword fishing.** Target queries like “too confusing” or “simple alternative.”
37. **YouTube complaint remix.** Record reaction shorts showing a cleaner version of a product people are venting about.
